Case Study - UTS Research Data Repository

Status: Live

A searchable portal for discovering and accessing public datasets by UTS researchers. Datasets are described with RO-Crates and published either through the University’s institutional research data management system, or direct import from research storage devices for very large datasets. Datasets are stored in an OCFL repository and indexed and published using the [Oni] data portal platform.

The UTS data repository is tightly integrated with the UTS Research Data Management Platform “Stash” which is an instance of ReDBox - an application funded by the Australian National Data Service (now [ARDC]).
