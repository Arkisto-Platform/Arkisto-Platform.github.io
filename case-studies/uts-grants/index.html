Case Study - UTS Successful Grants Repository

Status: Live in July 2020 (UTS staff only)

A searchable repository of successful grant applications, made available by the UTS Research Office to UTS research staff for professional development - as well as the basis for research projects to improve research performance. The Successful Grants Repository shows the use of Oni as a platform for indexing document collections with strict access control.
